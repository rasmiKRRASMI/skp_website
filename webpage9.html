Welcome to Our School The school was founded in 1910 by K.P. Narayanan Nambiar, an educator, social reformer and freedom fighter. The school was established as a primary school (first standard to fifth standard) in Parangot of Kayaralam Village and was moved to its current location in Arimbra of Mayyil Grama Panchayat in 1919.

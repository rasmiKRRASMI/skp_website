M E S KALLADI COLLEGE

MANNARAKAD

welcome to our college, M.E.S. Kalladi College, Mannarkkad is the proud symbol of the Muslim Educational Society, Calicut to the cause of higher education in Kerala, especially to the educationally backward areas of Malabar. This is the first college started by the MES and is also the Felicious outcome the noble endeavor of the late Kalladi Cheriya Kunhammed Sahib and the local public. College is located in a vast area, a major part of which was donated by the late Janab Valayadi Kunhayammu Haji. The campus is situated in the Mannarkkad Panchayath on a beautiful hillock in the Palakkad-Calicut National Highway and is bordered by the perennial Kunthipuzha and fortressed by panoramic Attapadi hills .

MANNAMPATTA

Welcome to Our College Velluthuruthi Thazathu Bhattathirippad college is located at Sreekrishnapuram in Palakkad District. The college is named after V.T. Bhattathiripad who is acknowledged as one of the pioneers of social revolution in Kerala. Sreekrishnapuram V. T. Bhathathirippad College, under the management of 'Sree Sankara Trust' started functioining in December 1982. Situated on the hilltop, the college is in the midst of the lush landscape. The college campus with its serene surroundings provide a wonderful learning ambiance for the aspiring students.

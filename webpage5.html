KUNDURKUNNU

Welcome to Our School T.S.N.M. Higher Secondary School, Kundurkunnu incarnates the philanthropic spirit of its founder Late T.S. Namboothiripad. Welfare of all, irrespective of their caste, creed or social and financial backgrounds was his sole motto when he founded an LP School at Kundurkunnu in 1948, now known as V.P.A.U.P. School. T.S.N.M. High School was started in the year 1962 which was upgraded to a Higher Secondary School in 2010. The Higher Secondary section is a young institution but with a rich history of 65 years. Located in a calm and serene place, the school has already established itself as a leading higher secondary school both in academic and in co- curricular activities. It is fully equipped with most modern infrastructure and a dedicated faculty. The school marches ahead with its full commitment to mould generations responsible to humanity with social commitment while being able to face the challenges of the modern world with full confidence. T.S.N.M. Higher Secondary School is situated at Kundurkunnu, Thachanattukara Panchayath, Palakkad District. It is about 3 Km from Kodakkad Junction along NH 213 (Perinthalmanna - Mannarkkad road), 4 Km from Kottapuram Junction along Mannarkkad - Cherpulassery road.

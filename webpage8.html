SREENARAYANA COLLEGE OF TEACHER EDUCATION

KOTTAPPURAM

welcome to our college, Sree Narayana Campus of Teacher Education, Kottapuram was named after the great saint philosopher and social reformer, Sree Narayana Guru and located in Palakkad state of Kerala. This college is run by Swami Swaswatheekananda trust for charity and education. The objective of college is to provide education for the prospective teachers and aim at forming socially committed intellectually competent, secular minded, truthful. The total campus area is 4.62 Acres and builds up area 16409.64 sq. ft. The college hostel is only for Ladies with intake capacity of 30 students and college has all necessary facilities. The college will protect each student to actualize his/her potential, to become efficient, competent, responsible and dedicated to the profession of teaching and to the community..
